PRIVACY POLICY - HIIT Timer Last updated: 27.10.2025 1. INTRODUCTION HIIT Timer app respects user privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it. 2. DATA COLLECTED BY THE APP The App stores the following data LOCALLY on your device: - Workout profiles (times, rounds, profile names) - App settings (volume, preferences) IMPORTANT: All data is stored only locally on your device. The App does NOT send any data to external servers. 3. PERMISSIONS The App requires the following permissions: - VIBRATE - vibration feedback during workout phase changes - FOREGROUND_SERVICE (optional) - running in background during workouts 4. PERSONAL DATA The App does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. 5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES The App does NOT use third-party services, analytics, or advertisements. 6. DATA SECURITY All data is stored locally on your device and protected by Android's security system. 7. USER RIGHTS You can at any time: - Delete all data by uninstalling the app - Delete individual profiles in the app settings 8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update this policy.
